How To Use Sharp

Sharp is really easy to use!



Step 1) Pick the key you would like your progression to be in.




Step 2) Choose the progression you would like to modify.




Step 3) Hit the Create button, and now you have your new progression!




Step 4) If you aren't sure what you want to choose, pick a random key and hit the random button to get a surprise!



About

Sharp is an easy-to-use app that is perfect for beginner guitarists. All you have to do is pick a key and a progression while we do the rest of the work for you. Whether you are looking for a new sound or have hit a creative spark, Sharp will be able to assist you.

This app represents our passion for music and creativity. Programming, like music, is a form of self expression and is a reflection of who we are. This project is the culmination of our work as a team, and we hope that you enjoy it as much as we did making it!
